Social Media Specialist

Job description

Status: Permanent – Full-time
Location: Montréal – Griffintown

At Trustnet Financial, we pride ourselves in offering the highest possible quality of customer experience. We are looking for a person who thrives in a team environment, who is passionate, driven and who yearns to grow personally and professionally. This person will become part of a team that thrives on the new challenges presented by a fast-paced and constantly evolving environment. The chosen candidate will possess a keen eye for detail and will come up with innovative ideas that will contribute to the success of both the company and the customer experience.

The mission of Trustnet Financial’s Marketing department is to create, optimize and maintain the company’s various Websites to increase their customer base and conversions into transactions. The main responsibility of the person who will be chosen for this position will be to promote the company’s Websites on social media in order to optimize their visibility.

Roles and responsibilities

  • Create and manage Trustnet Financial’s social media strategies and campaigns
  • Develop and produce Trustnet Financial’s voice and deploy it over all social media channels to create engagement
  • Develop and manage all Trustnet Financial content destined to social media platforms, including newsletters, marketing campaigns, blogs, daily posts and special promotions
  • Work closely with the User Experience team in order to maximize the use of social channels with regards to customer requests
  • Analyze, examine and produce reports on the efficiency of the campaigns and the tools used, including satisfaction analyses
  • Carry out the proper follow-ups and reports on the metrics directly linked to marketing and promotions, including Web analytics and social media statistics
  • Act as the administrator on the various social media platforms
  • Stay on top of the latest trends in the industry
  • Carry out other activities or related duties
  • Comply with the procedures and standards in place
  • Meet the department’s objectives
  • Complete the required training to acquire the necessary skills to carry out their duties

Skills and qualifications

  • Superior communication skills including verbal and written expression with great attention to detail
  • Superior spoken and written English skills, knowledge of a second language would be considered an advantage
  • Dynamic, creative, strategic and innovative thinker oriented toward results, able to self-motivate
  • Strong presence on social media
  • In-depth knowledge of digital marketing
  • Ability to create detailed reports
  • Versatility and the capacity to adapt to a rapidly evolving environment
  • Autonomy and ability to manage one’s own time
  • Superior computer and Web skills
  • Familiar with the usual business software such as Microsoft Office (Word, PowerPoint, Excel), Gmail, Google Drive

Training and work experience

  • College or university degree, or any relevant combination of training and work experience
  • At least 2 years work experience in social media
  • Solid experience in copy writing and content development
  • Proven experience in digital marketing, social media and email marketing
  • Experience with social media metrics tools such as Hootsuite
  • Any experience in the financial sector will be considered an advantage

Advantages

  • Competitive salary
  • Advancement opportunities
  • Social benefits
  • An ideal work environment!
