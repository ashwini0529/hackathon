Customer Experience
Advisor

Job description

Status: Permanent / Temporary – Full-time / Part-time
Location: Montréal – Griffintown

At Trustnet Financial, we pride ourselves in offering the highest possible quality of customer experience. We are looking for a person who thrives in a team environment, who is passionate, driven and who yearns to grow personally and professionally. This person will become part of a team that thrives on the new challenges presented by a fast-paced and constantly evolving environment. The chosen candidate will possess a keen eye for detail and will come up with innovative ideas that will contribute to the success of both the company and the customer experience.

The mission of Trustnet Financial’s Customer Experience department is to provide our customers with an exemplary and constantly optimized customer experience by implementing the very best customer service practices. The main responsibilities of the person who will be chosen for this position will be to adequately communicate with our customers, provide them with superior support and fulfill their requests. The successful Customer Experience Advisor carefully listens to the customer under any circumstance and is proactive toward them in order to surpass their expectations.

Roles and responsibilities

  • Provide impeccable support to Trustnet Financial’s customers on the phone, live chat, email and SMS
  • Thoroughly research customer files and ensure the appropriate follow-up
  • Identify the customer’s needs and prioritize them
  • Fully understand and resolve customer complaints
  • Answer customer support-related messages on the company’s various social media channels
  • Carry out other activities or related duties required by the supervisor or manager
  • Comply with the procedures and standards in place
  • Meet the department’s objectives
  • Complete the required training to acquire the necessary skills to carry out their duties

Skills and qualifications

  • Highly oriented toward customer service
  • Keen eye for detail
  • Superior interpersonal and customer service skills
  • Versatility and the capacity to adapt to a rapidly evolving environment
  • Autonomy and ability to manage one’s own time
  • Ability to rigorously document all information received from customers
  • Superior computer and Web skills
  • Superior listening and call treatment skills
  • Superior spoken and written English skills, knowledge of a second language would be considered an advantage
  • Familiar with the usual business software such as Microsoft Office (Word, PowerPoint, Excel), Gmail, Google Drive

 

Training and work experience

  • College degree in a relevant discipline, or any relevant combination of training and work experience
  • At least one year of work experience in customer service

 

Specific experience required

  • Customer service experience in a call center environment

 

Preferred type of experience

  • Experience in the banking industry, collections, fraud, marketing or technical support will be considered an advantage
  • Experience in using a customer relations management software (CRM) such as Salesforce will be considered an advantage

 

Advantages

  • Competitive salary
  • Advancement opportunities
  • Social benefits
  • An ideal work environment!
