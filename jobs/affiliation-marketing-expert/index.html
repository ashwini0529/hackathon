Affiliation Marketing Expert

Job description

Status: Permanent – Full-time
Location: Montréal – Griffintown

At Trustnet Financial, we pride ourselves in offering the highest possible quality of customer experience. We are looking for a person who thrives in a team environment, who is passionate, driven and who yearns to grow personally and professionally. This person will become part of a team that thrives on the new challenges presented by a fast-paced and constantly evolving environment. The chosen candidate will possess a keen eye for detail and will come up with innovative ideas that will contribute to the success of both the company and the customer experience.

The mission of Trustnet’s Marketing department is to create, optimize and maintain the company’s various Websites to increase their customer base and conversions into transactions. The main responsibility of the person who will be chosen for this position will be to establish affiliations with other Websites in order to increase the company’s customer base and optimize its profitability.

Roles and responsibilities

  • Elaborate and implement link acquisition initiatives through affiliation programs
  • Maintain the existing affiliation programs
  • Produce profitability and comparative reports on the company’s affiliation programs
  • Stay on top of the latest trends in the industry
  • Carry out other activities or related duties
  • Comply with the procedures and standards in place
  • Meet the department’s objectives
  • Complete the required training to acquire the necessary skills to carry out their duties

Skills and qualifications

  • Superior communication skills including verbal and written expression with great attention to detail and special attention to one’s tone of voice
  • Versatility and the capacity to adapt to a rapidly evolving environment
  • Superior spoken and written English skills, knowledge of a second language would be considered an advantage
  • Ability to produce return on investment analyses and to constantly identify the best strategies to improve said return on investment
  • Autonomy and ability to manage one’s own time
  • Keen eye for detail and highly developed organizational skills
  • Superior ability to carry out several tasks at once while meeting deadlines
  • Familiar with the usual business software such as Microsoft Office (Word, PowerPoint, Excel), Gmail, Google Drive

 

Training and work experience

  • Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, or any relevant combination of training and work experience

 

Specific experience required

  •  At least 3 years of work experience in affiliation programs
  • Familiar with Microsoft Office (Word, PowerPoint, Excel)

 

Preferred type of experience

  •  Any experience in the financial sector will be considered an advantage

 

Advantages

  • Competitive salary
  • Advancement opportunities
  • Social benefits
  • An ideal work environment!
