Web Marketing Specialist

Job description

Status: Permanent – Full-time
Location: Montréal – Griffintown

At Trustnet Financial, we pride ourselves in offering the highest possible quality of customer experience. We are looking for a person who thrives in a team environment, who is passionate, driven and who yearns to grow personally and professionally. This person will become part of a team that thrives on the new challenges presented by a fast-paced and constantly evolving environment. The chosen candidate will possess a keen eye for detail and will come up with innovative ideas that will contribute to the success of both the company and the customer experience.

The mission of Trustnet’s Marketing department is to create, optimize and maintain the company’s various Websites to increase their customer base and conversions into transactions. The main responsibility of the person who will be chosen for this position will be to develop and execute all Web marketing strategies and campaigns to increase the visibility of the company’s Websites.

Roles and responsibilities

  • Implement and optimize online marketing initiatives in order to increase customer acquisition
  • Implement and maintain SEM strategies (SEO and SEA)
  • Analyze and optimize search engine-related marketing initiatives
  • Working with social media specialists, implement, develop and manage social network channels, blogs, e-blasts in order to maximize leads and to promote the company
  • Carry out analyses and produce weekly and monthly digital marketing and leads reports
  • Create HTML-based emails, download leads lists, send out those email blasts using the automated marketing system and produce reports on each blast
  • Monitor Website performance using Web analytics tools
  • Develop and implement new and creative social media campaigns to promote each Trustnet Financial Website
  • Contribute to the evolution of the current content strategy to optimize Web content development
  • Participate in product development and promotion strategies
  • Help with writing and proofreading the copy of sales and marketing tools such as articles, presentations, guides and others
  • Monitor projects, including their documentation and schedule
  • Prepare the company’s press releases, newsletters and contests
  • Update the news and blog posts over the company’s Websites
  • Create innovative approaches in content creation and promotion
  • Research, develop and execute innovative marketing initiatives to support new services
  • Provide clear and concise content for the company’s Websites and social media channels
  • Stay on top of the latest trends in the industry
  • Carry out other activities or related duties
  • Comply with the procedures and standards in place
  • Meet the department’s objectives
  • Complete the required training to acquire the necessary skills to carry out their duties

Skills and qualifications

  • Creative
  • Superior communication skills including verbal and written expression with great attention to detail
  • Superior spoken and written English skills, knowledge of a second language would be considered an advantage
  • Proven ability to develop and produce messages and tools specifically for a demanding audience (videos, customer testimonials, guides, presentations, demos, etc.) and that will be used on the Web, during events and in promotional campaigns
  • A thorough understanding of SEM is a necessity
  • Versatility and the capacity to adapt to a rapidly evolving environment
  • Ability to produce return on investment analyses and to constantly identify the best strategies to improve said return on investment
  • Autonomy and ability to manage one’s own time
  • Keen eye for detail and highly developed organizational skills
  • Superior ability to carry out several tasks at once while meeting deadlines
  • Superior computer and Web skills
  • Familiar with the usual business software such as Microsoft Office (Word, PowerPoint, Excel), Gmail, Google Drive

Training and work experience

  • Bachelor’s degree in marketing, Web Design / Development, or any relevant combination of training and work experience

Specific experience required

  • At least 5 years of professional experience in Web Marketing
  • Experience in Website development (landing pages, microsites, templates, basic HTML an CSS)
  • Experience using email marketing software
  • At least 2 years experience in SEO and SEM
  • Experience using online Web analytics tools (Google Analytics, Website Optimizer, Keyword Tool, URL Builder)
  • Experience using social media (blogs, Twitter, LinkedIn, Google+, Facebook, YouTube)
  • Familiar with Microsoft Office (Word, PowerPoint, Excel)
  • Familiar with Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop)
  • Sensitive to user experience and design

Specific experience required

  • Familiar with Web services, APIs and data stream will be considered an advantage
  • Experience using Hootsuite will be considered an advantage
  • Any experience in the financial sector will be considered an advantage

 

Advantages

  • Competitive salary
  • Advancement opportunities
  • Social benefits
  • An ideal work environment!
