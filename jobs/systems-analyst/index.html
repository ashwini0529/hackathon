Systems Analyst

Job description

Status: Permanent – Full-time
Location: Montréal – Griffintown

At Trustnet Financial, we pride ourselves in offering the highest possible quality of customer experience. We are looking for a person who thrives in a team environment, who is passionate, driven and who yearns to grow personally and professionally. This person will become part of a team that thrives on the new challenges presented by a fast-paced and constantly evolving environment. The chosen candidate will possess a keen eye for detail and will come up with innovative ideas that will contribute to the success of both the company and the customer experience.

The mission of this position with Trustnet Financial is to optimize, maintain, analyze and test automation for our loan management system in Salesforce. Including loan applications, member’s area and websites. This position touches as well network systems, IT, Internet Security protocols and anything related.

Roles and responsibilities

  • Manage the relationship between Trustnet Financial and its Loan Management System on a Salesforce platform.
  • Create and modify the specifications for the functionalities
  • Monitor and do the follow-up of the programming process
  • Perform quality control
  • Stay on top of the latest trends in the industry
  • Work in collaboration with all stakeholders
  • Carry out other activities or related duties required by the Directors
  • Comply with the procedures and standards in place
  • Meet the department’s objectives
  • Complete the required training to acquire the necessary skills to carry out their duties

Skills and qualifications

  • Extensive knowledge of several of programming languages
  • Software, Mobile and Web development skills and experience
  • Testing, Deployment and Maintenance experience
  • Query, Ms Project, Axure knowledge and experience
  • Superior computer and Web skills
  • Superior Salesforce knowledge
  • Financial Services experience
  • Keen eye for detail and highly developed organizational skills
  • Superior ability to carry out several tasks at once while meeting deadlines
  • Versatility and the capacity to adapt to a rapidly evolving environment
  • Autonomy and ability to manage one’s own time
  • Team Leadership
  • Superior spoken and written English skills, knowledge of a second language would be considered an advantage
  • Familiar with the usual business software such as Microsoft Office (Word, PowerPoint, Excel), Gmail, Google Drive

 

Training and work experience

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, engineering or a college degree in Computer Science with adequate work experience, or any relevant combination of training and work experience

 

Specific experience required

  • Software engineering experience, preferably between 2-6 years in product development.
  • Good with software design and requirement analysis.
  • Object oriented programming.
  • Salesforce, Force platform experience preferred.

 

Preferred type of experience

  • Salesforce certified
  • Any experience in the financial sector will be considered an advantage
  • Experience using Salesforce is a clear advantage
