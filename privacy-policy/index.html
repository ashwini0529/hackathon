Personal Information

Trustnet Financial uses personal information as required by state and federal regulators to assure we are promoting safe and responsible lending.

When you first apply for a Trustnet Financial loan, we ask you to create an account and then ask you to supply certain personal information. We collect, use, and disclose this information:

  • to verify (as much as reasonably possible) that it is you, rather than someone else, that is applying for the loan in your name
  • to determine your eligibility for the loan under the law and whether you can afford it
  • to maintain your account
  • to communicate with you about your account
  • to securely process the funding and repayment transactions to which you and we might agree
  • to provide financial services to customers;
  • to develop, manage and market products and services to meet the needs of customers;
  • to obtain credit information and credit reports from credit reporting agencies to assess credit history and credit worthiness and to confirm accuracy of information provided by individuals (including by contacting employers and personal references);
  • to confirm details of customer employment;
  • to contact customers for products and services that may be of interest;
  • to determine the eligibility of customers for different products and services that may be of interest;
  • to extract certain information for the purpose of generating statistics and analysis for purposes of conducting and improving our business;
  • to ensure a high standard of service to customers;
  • to meet legal and regulatory requirements;
  • to confirm the identity of a customer and to prevent and detect fraud; and
  • to collect debt that may become owed to GoDay by a customer.

The types of personal information we collect for these purposes can include:

  • basic profile information such as your name, address, phone number, email address, preferences, and other similar information
  • identifying information such as social security number, date of birth, or other information
  • information such as employment, income or similar information
  • transactional information such as including bank account numbers, routing numbers, debit card numbers or similar information

Trustnet Financial may also obtain more information about applicants from third parties to help us validate this information. We also collect tokens from online services and payment systems.

After you become a customer by taking out a Trustnet Financial loan, we use your information for transaction processing and to communicate with you.
